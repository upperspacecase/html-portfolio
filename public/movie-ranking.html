Tay's Favourite Movies of All Time

My Top 3 Movies

1. The Dark Knight

Directed by Christopher Nolan, The Dark Knight is a superhero film based on the DC Comics character Batman. The film stars Christian Bale as Batman, Heath Ledger as the Joker, and Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent. The Dark Knight is widely regarded as one of the best superhero movies of all time and is known for its dark and gritty tone, complex characters, and thrilling action sequences.

2. Inception

Inception is a science fiction film written and directed by Christopher Nolan. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as a professional thief who steals information by entering the dreams of his targets. Inception is known for its mind-bending plot, stunning visual effects, and thought-provoking themes about reality and the nature of dreams.
